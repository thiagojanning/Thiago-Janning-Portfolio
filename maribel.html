Studios Maribel

Studios Maribel is a photography studio located in Oeiras, Portugal, specializing in both event and studio photography, as well as analog photography services. The website is thoughtfully structured into four key categories — Studio, Labs, Events, and Products — plus a dedicated Contact section, to streamline navigation and provide a comprehensive overview of their services.

  • What I Did
  • UI

Built on the Squarespace platform, the website is designed for ease of use, allowing the owners to effortlessly update and manage content. Carefully curated photographs enhance each category and subcategory, enriching the visual experience and aligning with the studio’s aesthetic. This design approach not only showcases their work but also ensures a user-friendly interface for visitors.
