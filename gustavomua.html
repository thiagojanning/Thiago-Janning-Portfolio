Gustavo MUA

Gustavo is a brazilian makeup artist living in Portugal. The Instagram profile @gustavo.mua is a professional account that features his makeup looks, close-ups and product pictures. Content production is based on the building pillars of the personal brand Gustavo MUA and all ideas are structured considering these pillars. Engagement is stimulated by producing stories related to profile content, hashtags, and interaction with specific profiles.

The profile features content focused on the Brazilian and Portuguese audiences and the feed is organized in three sections (columns) for a more appealing look. The first consists of eye close-ups, and the other two intercalate lips and full faces with products.
