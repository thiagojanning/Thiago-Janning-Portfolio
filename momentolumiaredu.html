Momento LumiarEdu

Momento LumiarEdu was a strategic event designed to engage potential franchisees and educational institutions interested in adopting the Lumiar methodology or utilizing the Mosaico platform.

The landing page for this event aimed to convey key details and drive registrations. To set it apart from Lumiar’s traditional communications, the brand’s signature blue was used as the primary color, creating a fresh and distinctive visual identity. The traditional yellow served as a secondary color, providing a vibrant contrast and reinforcing brand recognition.
