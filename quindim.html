Quindim Creative Studio

Quindim Creative Studio is a dynamic branding and editorial design agency based in Florianópolis. Co-founded by designers Isabel Wermuth and Thiago Janning, the studio is renowned for its distinctive approach to visual identity and design.

Our branding project for Quindim was crafted to evoke a classic manuscript feel, characterized by varying letter thicknesses and fluid, organic forms.

The logo’s typography features distinctive lines that rise and fall, particularly noticeable in the letters “n” and “m,” to convey a sense of movement and creativity.

For the tagline, we chose Museo in weight 300—a free, serif font created by Dutch designer Jos Buivenga. Museo’s organic curves and elegant form complement the logo’s typography, creating a cohesive and harmonious visual identity that reflects the studio’s creative essence. The font adds a bit more flair and depth to the descriptions, aiming to better capture the essence of the studio’s branding project and the thoughtful design choices involved.
