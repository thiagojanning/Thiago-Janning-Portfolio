The Quindim's logo sought to keep a manuscript appearance, with differences in the thickness of letters and the use of organic forms. The typography lines that go up and down and end of the letters "n" and "m" also give the idea of movement. The typography used in the tagline is Museo in the weight 300, a free typography developed by dutch Jos Buivenga. Museo is a serif font and has organic forms, harmonizing with the logo's typography curves.
