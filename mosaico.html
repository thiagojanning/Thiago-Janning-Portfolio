Mosaico

Mosaico is an innovative EdTech platform designed to enhance and empower the education community through a comprehensive technological ecosystem. Focused on active learning, Mosaico simplifies the mapping and assessment of competencies, enabling educators to better track and support student development.

The platform features a visual representation of student profiles and progress through disc-shaped matrixes. This visual scheme, which inspired the logo’s symbol, uses color-coded segments to illustrate the competencies students have developed, both individually and in groups, throughout their learning journey. Each color on the disc corresponds to specific areas of achievement, providing a clear and engaging way to monitor and assess educational growth.

The logo highlights Mosaico’s core mission and the visual design elements of the platform, providing a comprehensive overview of its functionality and design inspiration.
