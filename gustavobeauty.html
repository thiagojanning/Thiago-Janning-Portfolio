Gustavo Beauty

Gustavo is a brazilian hairdresser living in Portugal. The Instagram profile @gustavobeauty is a professional account that features his work. The content production is based on 3 categories: Work pictures, Keune products and informative posts. Instagram stories and reels are produced as a complement to the feed content and as a way of engaging more users.

The serif font added to the chosen colors give the profile a contemporary look. For longer texts, a sans serif font was selected, which receives additional elements such as rectangles, lines and bold to create highlights in relevant information. The brand also has the reduced version “gb” to be used as a tag in photographs.
