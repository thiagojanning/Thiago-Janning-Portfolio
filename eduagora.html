Lumiar Educação para o Agora

Educação para o Agora (Education for now) was a campaign/movement by Lumiar Education focused on proposing creative and innovative solutions to transform education in the world.

  • What I Did
  • Branding
  • Social Media Assets
  • Stationery

In terms of the visual identity of the page, I followed the Lumiar Educação brand identity: the predominant use of yellow in contrast to blue, orange and green shapes. Photos and a series of the institutional illustrations were also used. The campaign’s target audience was extensive and ranged from students and parents to school managers, teachers and government members.
