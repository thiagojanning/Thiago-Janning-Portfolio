Bistrolar

The brand for the "Coordenação de Estágios do Curso Cooperativo de Engenharia de Materiais da UFSC", as well as the was the brand DNA the Purpose were a team project conducted during my intership at LOGO UFSC.

The methodology used for the project was the TXM Branding, which covers the whole process of brand creation and its strategies. It is a set of tools which through co-creation, involves the client in all the steps.

  • What I Did
  • Branding
  • Social Media Assets
  • Stationery

The Brand DNA was defined according to the Brand DNA Process® as: Connected (Technical Concept), Cooperative (Resilient Concept), Stimulant (Emotional Concept), Virtuous (Marketing Concept) and Partner (Integrative Concept)

The Brand Purpose was defined as "To reduce the boundaries between Academy and the Market, stimulating the cooperative formation, contributing to the development of professionals of excellence".
